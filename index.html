Commonly abbreviated as SF, Street Fighter is a fighting video game franchise by Capcom. The second game in the series is credited with establishing many of the conventions of the one-on-one fighting genre. The game's playable characters originate from different countries around the world, each with a unique fighting style. It is Capcom's second best-selling franchise, having had significant success worldwide. The first game in the series was released in 1987.


How to Play:

Your Player will be set to Ken. Unlike your conventional arcade fighting game, both players will start off with a health value of 200.

  • Both player and computer will start off with 200 Health
  • Player and computer have a option to heal or attack. Health is permitted to surpass the starting health so plan strategically
  • There is a chance to miss and deal "0" damage
  • A round is won when the opponents health reaches 0 or below
  • The game is like survival mode. Die once and the game is over
  • Each "new-game" the player will get to act first
  • On the other hand if you knock out the opponent, the next challenger gets to attack you first so be mindful of your health
  • For fun you can click on ken to unleash the well reknowned "Hadouken"
